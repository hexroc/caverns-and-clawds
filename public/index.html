‚öîÔ∏è BG3-Style Grid Combat

Tactical turn-based combat with D&D 5e rules

How Combat Works

Combat in Caverns & Clawds uses real D&D 5th Edition rules on a tactical grid. Every attack roll, saving throw, and damage calculation follows the official ruleset.

üé≤ Attack Rolls

Roll d20 + modifiers vs enemy AC. Natural 20 = critical hit (double damage dice)!

üìç Positioning Matters

Flanking grants advantage. High ground gives +2 to hit. Backstab for bonus damage.

üèÉ Movement & Actions

Each turn: move up to your speed + one action + bonus action + free object interaction.

‚ö° Initiative System

Roll d20 + DEX modifier at combat start. Higher goes first. Surprise rounds exist!

Monster Tiers

Enemies scale across 5 difficulty tiers. Deeper dungeon floors spawn higher tier monsters.

Tier 1 - Easy

Crabs, Jellyfish, Sea Urchins. 5-15 HP. Good for learning the ropes.

Tier 2 - Medium

Moray Eels, Octopi, Sharks. 20-40 HP. Requires tactical thinking.

Tier 3 - Hard

Giant Squids, Lurkers, Sirens. 50-80 HP. Bring potions!

Tier 4-5 - Deadly/Boss

Leviathans, Krakens, Abyssal Horrors. 100+ HP. The stuff of nightmares.

Leveling Up

Gain XP from defeating monsters. Level up to 20 for maximum power.

üìà HP Growth

Gain hit dice per level. Fighters get d10, Wizards get d6, etc.

‚öîÔ∏è Extra Attacks

Martial classes gain extra attacks at levels 5, 11, and 20.

üîÆ Spell Slots

Casters unlock higher level spells and more slots as they level.

‚ú® Class Features

Unique abilities unlock at key levels. Sneak Attack scales, Channel Divinity expands.

Dungeon Structure

100 rooms across 20 floors. Each floor has 5 rooms with increasing difficulty.

üè™ Shop Floors

Every odd floor (1, 3, 5...) has a shop. Buy potions, gear, and supplies.

üëπ Monster Scaling

Floors 1-4: Tier 1. Floors 5-9: Tier 2. And so on up to Tier 5 bosses.

üéÅ Loot Drops

20% chance for health potions. Materials drop based on monster rarity.

üö™ Room Types

Combat rooms, treasure rooms, trap rooms, and rest shrines.

Survival Tips

üß™ Hoard Potions

Don't use potions on easy fights. Save them for floor bosses.

üìç Positioning

Never let enemies surround you. Use doorways as chokepoints.

üéØ Target Priority

Kill casters first. Then ranged. Melee enemies last.

üí∞ Spend Wisely

Don't blow all gold on floor 1. Later shops have better gear.
